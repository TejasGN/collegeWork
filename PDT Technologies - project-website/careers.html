Hiring Process

1. Talent Acquisition Outreach

HR flags potential candidates & starts a conversation.

2. Telephonic Screening

Talk to a senior team member to discuss your background, our needs and establish a mutual fit. Get ready to deep dive into complex tech and business cases.

3. Case Study (Business & Product Roles)

A chance for business and product candidates to showcase their detail thinking on real world business challenges.

4. Onsite Interview

Spend the day at one of our offices, meet multiple leads across teams to get a better feel of our people and culture. Get hands-on with technical problems or business strategies.

5. Management Round

Meet senior management, discuss Technology, IT industry, Market and how you can make a difference.
