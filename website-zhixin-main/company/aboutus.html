ABOUT US

Who we are

Founded on August 8, 2019, Hefei Zhixin semiconductor co., ltd. is a high-tech semiconductor company focusing on reliable, secure and safe automotive solutions.

With its headquarter in Hefei, Zhixin has R&D centers in Nanjing, Shanghai, Suzhou, and Tianjin.

At Zhixin, we are committed to provide secure, robust, and higly-reliable automotive solutions, which are mainly used in automotive industry, industry applications, such as car body applications, and enginerring motor control.
